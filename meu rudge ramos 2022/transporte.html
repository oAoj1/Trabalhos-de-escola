Let's take a walk!, now we're going to show you how Rudge Ramos' means of transport work:

Rudge Ramos is a neighborhood considered as a connection through public transport to the main neighborhoods of São Bernardo do Campo, but it also connects the city to other neighboring municipalities: São Paulo, Santo André, São Caetano do Sul and Diadema. In 2016, the implementation of bus lanes and the construction of a bus terminal at the end of Avenida Dr. Rudge Ramos, with the aim of receiving the first station of the extinct line 18 Bronze, which was replaced by another type of modal, the BRT, but the construction of line 20 Rosa is already under geological studies and the Rudge Ramos neighborhood will have two stations, one in the center near Largo São João Batista and Rua Afonsina.
