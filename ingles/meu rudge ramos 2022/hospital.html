HOSPITALS

We open this page talking about the public and private health networks in the neighborhood, starting with:

The neighbourhood has two UBSs, one located on Rua Angela Tomé and the other on Rua Aura, and the Neomater and Intermédica hospitals.

The UBS on Angela Tomé Street has an electrocardiograph, a pulmonary resuscitator/ambu and a dental team, as well as offering a regional dental laboratory, psychosocial care, prenatal care for normal risk and other services.

The one on Aura Street, also known as Caminho do Mar UBS, has a Dental X-Ray, Electrocardiograph and a Dental Team, as well as offering Approach and Treatment for Smokers, Electrocardiographic Examination, Asynchronous Teleconsulting and other services.
